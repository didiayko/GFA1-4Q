Waves for Water

"Do what you love and help along the way."

Waves for Water (W4W) os a nonprofit organization in the Philippines with a goal to provide access to clean water for everyone who needs it. They also coordinate and execute natural disaster relief efforts around the world.

I chose to support this organization because having access to clean water is very important. Contaminated water can cause transmission of many diseases such as cholera, diarrheoa, hepatitis A, polio, and more. Over the years, the quality of water in the Philippines has worsened, especially in densely populated areas and ragions of industrial and agricultural activity. 52% of the population of the Philippines lack access to a safely managed water source.
